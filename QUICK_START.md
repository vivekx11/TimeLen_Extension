# TimeLens Quick Start Guide



### Step 1: Install (1 minute)
1. Open Chrome and go to `chrome://extensions/`
2. Enable "Developer mode" (top-right toggle)
3. Click "Load unpacked" and select the TimeLens folder
4. Done! Icon appears in toolbar

### Step 2: Start Tracking (30 seconds)
1. Click the TimeLens icon
2. Start browsing normally
3. That's it! Tracking happens automatically

### Step 3: View Your Stats (30 seconds)
1. Click TimeLens icon anytime
2. See your total time and top websites
3. Watch your productivity insights

---

## 🎯 Enable Focus Mode (Optional)

Want to limit time on distracting sites?

1. Open TimeLens popup
2. Toggle "Focus Mode" ON
3. Click "Manage Limits"
4. Add a website (e.g., "youtube.com")
5. Set time limit in minutes (e.g., 60)
6. Click "Save"

Now when you exceed the limit, you'll be redirected to a friendly reminder page!

## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Does it track incognito tabs?
A: No, Chrome extensions don't have access to incognito by default.

### Q: Where is my data stored?
A: Locally on your device only. Never sent anywhere.

### Q: Does it slow down my browser?
A: No, TimeLens uses minimal resources (<5MB memory).

### Q: Can I see history from yesterday?
A: Not yet - currently shows today only. Historical data coming in v1.1!

### Q: What happens at midnight?
A: Data automatically resets for a fresh start.

### Q: Can I export my data?
A: Not yet - export feature coming in v1.1!

### Q: Does it work on mobile?
A: No, Chrome extensions are desktop-only.

### Q: Is my data private?
A: Yes! Everything stays on your device. No servers, no tracking.

## 🐛 Troubleshooting

### Not Tracking?
1. Check extension is enabled
2. Reload extension
3. Close and reopen Chrome

### Icon Missing?
1. Go to chrome://extensions/
2. Find TimeLens
3. Click "Details"
4. Ensure "Show in toolbar" is enabled

### Focus Mode Not Working?
1. Verify Focus Mode is toggled ON
2. Check domain spelling (no "www.")
3. Ensure time limit is set
4. Reload extension
